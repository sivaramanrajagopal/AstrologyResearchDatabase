How Recommendations Are Calculated
  • 10th House Analysis: Planet in/aspecting 10th house (career house) in D1 and D10
  • 10th Lord Position: Strength and placement of 10th house lord
  • Planetary Strength: Exaltation, own sign, friendly signs in D1 and D10
  • Ashtakavarga: SAV points in 10th house (higher = better career prospects)
  • Dasha Periods: Current and upcoming planetary periods for timing
  • Yogas: Special planetary combinations (Raja Yoga, Dhana Yoga, etc.)
Research Notes

This analysis is based on classical Vedic astrology texts including Brihat Parashara Hora Shastra, Jataka Parijata, and Phaladeepika. The career recommendations combine multiple factors across D1 (birth chart), D10 (career-specific divisional chart), Dasha timing, and Ashtakavarga strength for comprehensive analysis.
